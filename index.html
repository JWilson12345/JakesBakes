The cookie

One proper cookie. No unnecessary extras.

Each cookie starts with butter cooked until golden and nutty, then gets mixed with dark brown sugar, vanilla and two kinds of chocolate. The result is lightly crisp around the edge, soft and chewy through the middle and finished with a little flaky salt.

What goes into them

Simple ingredients, used properly.

  • Browned salted butter
  • Plain and bread flour
  • Dark brown and white sugar
  • Eggs and vanilla
  • Dark chocolate
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • Flaky sea salt
