The cookie

The Biscoff Royale.

Each cookie starts with butter cooked until golden and nutty, then gets mixed with brown sugar, vanilla, chopped milk chocolate and chocolate chips. Biscoff biscuit pieces are pressed into the top before the cookies are finished with a Nutella-and-milk drizzle and a milk chocolate chip.

What goes into them

Simple ingredients, used properly.

  • Browned salted butter
  • Plain and bread flour
  • Brown and white sugar
  • Eggs and vanilla
  • Milk chocolate
  • Chocolate chips
  • Biscoff biscuit pieces
  • Nutella and milk drizzle
