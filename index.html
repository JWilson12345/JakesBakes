The base cookie

Brown Butter Chocolate Chip.

Each cookie starts with British butter cooked until golden and nutty, then gets mixed with dark brown sugar, Madagascan vanilla, milk chocolate chunks and milk chocolate chips. The Biscoff Royale builds from this same base with biscuit and drizzle on top.

What goes into them

Simple ingredients, used properly.

  • Browned British butter
  • Plain and strong white flour
  • Dark brown and granulated sugar
  • Free range egg and Madagascan vanilla
  • Milk chocolate chunks
  • Milk chocolate chips
  • Biscoff biscuit on The Biscoff Royale
  • Nutella-milk drizzle on The Biscoff Royale
