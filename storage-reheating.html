Storage and Reheating

Keeping your cookies at their best.

Storage

Store in an airtight container at room temperature and enjoy within three to five days for the best texture.

Freezing

Freeze in suitable airtight packaging. Defrost fully before eating or warming.

Reheating

Remove all packaging first. Warm gently in short intervals and check carefully, as appliance strengths vary.
