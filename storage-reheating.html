Storage and Reheating

Keeping your cookies at their best.

Storage

Store in an airtight container and enjoy within three to five days for the best texture. Confirm final guidance before launch.

Freezing

Cookies can be frozen in suitable airtight packaging. Follow the final storage instructions supplied with your order.

Reheating

Warm gently in short intervals and check carefully, as appliance strengths vary. Remove all packaging first.
