How it works

From Friday preorder to Friday collection.

Weekly preorder

The weekly preorder opens every Friday. Orders stay open for 48 hours or until all 25 boxes have been reserved.

Choosing a box

Choose either four or six brown-butter chocolate-chip cookies and pay online.

Preparation

After preorders close, ingredients and orders are organised for the week. Baking begins from Wednesday.

Friday collection

Your box will be ready to collect the following Friday from Barnsley. Choose an available collection time during checkout.

When sold out

Once all 25 boxes are reserved, the weekly preorder closes. The next preorder opens the following Friday.
