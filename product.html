Product details

A thick brown-butter chocolate chip cookie with golden edges and a soft middle, topped with Biscoff biscuit, Nutella-milk drizzle and a milk chocolate chip.

Wheat flour, salted butter (milk), dark brown soft sugar, granulated sugar, milk chocolate (milk, soya), milk chocolate chips (milk, soya), free range egg, Madagascan vanilla extract, sodium bicarbonate, salt, Biscoff biscuit (wheat/gluten) and Nutella-milk drizzle (hazelnuts, milk).

Contains wheat/gluten, milk, egg, soya and hazelnuts. May contain peanuts and tree nuts depending on ingredient packaging. Prepared in a home kitchen where other allergens may be handled.
