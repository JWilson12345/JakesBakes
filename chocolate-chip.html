Product details

A brown-butter chocolate chip cookie with crisp golden edges and a soft, chewy centre.

Wheat flour (plain flour, strong white bread flour), salted butter (milk), dark brown soft sugar, granulated sugar, milk chocolate (milk, soya), milk chocolate chips (milk, soya), free range egg, Madagascan vanilla extract, sodium bicarbonate and salt.

Contains wheat/gluten, milk, egg and soya. May contain peanuts and tree nuts depending on ingredient packaging. Prepared in a home kitchen where other allergens may be handled.

Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Best enjoyed within three days, suitable for up to five days when stored correctly.

Enjoy at room temperature or warm gently before serving for a softer centre. Remove all packaging first.
