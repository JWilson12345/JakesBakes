Brown Butter Chocolate Chip

The base Jake's Bakes cookie: browned British butter, milk chocolate chunks and milk chocolate chips, baked with crisp golden edges and a soft, chewy centre.

Ingredients

Wheat Flour (Plain Flour, Strong White Bread Flour), Salted Butter (Milk), Dark Brown Soft Sugar, Granulated Sugar, Milk Chocolate (Milk, Soya), Milk Chocolate Chips (Milk, Soya), Free Range Egg, Madagascan Vanilla Extract, Raising Agent (Sodium Bicarbonate), Salt.

Allergens

  • Wheat / gluten
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Soya

May contain peanuts and tree nuts depending on ingredient packaging. No nuts are added to this product.

The Biscoff Royale

The Biscoff Royale uses the Brown Butter Chocolate Chip base, then adds Biscoff biscuit, Nutella-and-milk drizzle and a milk chocolate chip on top.

Base ingredients

Wheat Flour (Plain Flour, Strong White Bread Flour), Salted Butter (Milk), Dark Brown Soft Sugar, Granulated Sugar, Milk Chocolate (Milk, Soya), Milk Chocolate Chips (Milk, Soya), Free Range Egg, Madagascan Vanilla Extract, Raising Agent (Sodium Bicarbonate), Salt.

Additional ingredients

Biscoff biscuit (Wheat / gluten), Nutella (Hazelnuts, Milk) and milk chocolate chip topping (Milk, Soya).

Allergens

  • Wheat / gluten
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Soya
  • Hazelnuts

May contain peanuts and tree nuts depending on ingredient packaging.

Dietary and storage information

  • Suitable for vegetarians.
  • Not vegan, dairy free or gluten free.
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Keep in an airtight container after collection.
  • Best enjoyed within 3 days, suitable for up to 5 days when stored correctly.
