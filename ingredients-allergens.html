Ingredients and Allergens

Clear information before you order.

This is a provisional ingredient summary, not the final statutory ingredient declaration. The live site must use exact ingredient lists from the brands purchased, including compound ingredients within chocolate and vanilla products.

Draft ingredients

  • Salted butter
  • Plain wheat flour
  • Bread wheat flour
  • Bicarbonate of soda
  • Salt
  • Dark brown sugar
  • Granulated sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract or vanilla bean paste
  • Dark chocolate
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • Flaky sea salt

Likely allergens

  • Wheat / gluten
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Soya, depending on chocolate used

These cookies are prepared in a home kitchen where other allergens may be handled. The exact ingredient and allergen information will be confirmed using the products included in each batch. Please contact Jake before ordering if you have an allergy or intolerance.

Do not claim nut-free, peanut-free, gluten-free, dairy-free, vegan, suitable for severe allergies or free from cross-contamination unless final processes and labels support it.

The final vegetarian suitability will be confirmed once every ingredient brand has been selected.
