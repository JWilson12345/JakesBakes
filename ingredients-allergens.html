Ingredients and Allergens

Clear information before you order.

Jake's Bakes is a small home-baking business. If you have an allergy or intolerance, contact Jake before ordering so the current batch ingredients can be checked with you.

Ingredients

  • Salted butter
  • Plain wheat flour
  • Bread wheat flour
  • Bicarbonate of soda
  • Salt
  • Brown sugar
  • Granulated sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract or vanilla bean paste
  • Milk chocolate
  • Chocolate chips
  • Biscoff biscuit pieces
  • Nutella
  • Milk for the drizzle

Allergens

  • Wheat / gluten
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Hazelnuts
  • Soya may be present in chocolate and Biscoff ingredients

Cookies are prepared in a home kitchen where other allergens, including nuts and peanuts, may be handled. They are not suitable for anyone who needs a guaranteed allergen-free product.

Ingredient brands can change between batches. Order information will include the details needed for that week's bake.
