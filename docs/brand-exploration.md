# Brand Exploration

Temporary working name used throughout prototype: **Jake's Bakes**.

| Name | Feel | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Jake's Bakes | Personal, direct, local | Strong for founder-led home bakery; broad if products expand |
| Butter Crumb | Warm, food-led | Premium without sounding corporate |
| Butter & Crumb | Polished, established | Slightly more brandable, less personal |
| Jake's Cookie Co. | Clear, commercial | Less cosy than Jake's Bakes |
| Friday Crumbs | Memorable, preorder-led | Ties nicely to weekend drop model |
| The Weekend Cookie | Concept-led | Strong positioning, less local/personal |
| Brown Butter Bakehouse | Premium and descriptive | May imply broader bakery scale |
| Barnsley Cookie Co. | Local and clear | Strong local SEO, less personal |

Do not mix these names on the customer-facing site. Replace globally through `STORE_CONFIG.brandName` and Shopify theme settings later.
