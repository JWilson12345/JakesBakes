# Allergen Audit Needed

Do not publish final allergen claims until Jake confirms:

- Exact butter brand
- Exact flour
- Exact chocolate
- Exact Oreo product
- Exact Biscoff product
- Exact drizzle or sauce recipe
- Cross-contamination environment
- Shared kitchen equipment
- Packaging labels
- Ingredient substitutions
- Manufacturer may-contain statements

Likely allergens requiring verification include milk, wheat/gluten, eggs and soya. Additional allergens or traces may come from chocolate, biscuits, sauces, toppings or shared equipment.

Do not claim nut-free, peanut-free, gluten-free, vegan, halal, dairy-free or safe for severe allergies unless the final ingredients and process support it.
