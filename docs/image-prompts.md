## Homepage Hero

White cookie box with clear window, partially open, six thick brown-butter cookies, three launch flavours visible, box slightly off-centre, negative space for copy, soft daylight, neutral kitchen/parchment surface, natural crumbs, no logo, no text, believable scale.

## Chocolate Chip

One main 80g brown-butter chocolate-chip cookie in sharp focus, a few cookies behind it, thick but not oversized, golden edges, cracked surface, visible chocolate chunks, soft background, no excessive melted chocolate.

## Oreo

Brown-butter cookie with visible Oreo-style element, dark biscuit pieces integrated naturally, restrained cream drizzle, crumb detail, no distorted branding or impossible biscuit geometry.

## Biscoff

Brown-butter cookie with Biscoff-style centre, warm biscuit drizzle, fine crumb topping, realistic baked texture, no excessive liquid sauce.

## Broken Open

Hands naturally breaking one cookie in half, soft centre visible, realistic crumb, correct fingers, natural skin texture, clean food-safe presentation.

## Plate Scene

Several cookies on a simple ceramic plate, one in focus, warm natural light, minimal props, no unnecessary styling.

## Packaging

Plain white windowed cookie box with six cookies arranged neatly, no permanent graphic design, optional blank temporary sticker, honest small-business presentation.
