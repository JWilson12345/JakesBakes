About

Hi, I'm Jake.

Jake's Bakes started with a pretty simple idea: everyone deserves a proper cookie to enjoy at the weekend.

I bake from home in Barnsley and keep each weekly drop small enough to prepare properly. The cookies start with a brown-butter chocolate chip base, then you can keep it classic or choose The Biscoff Royale with Biscoff biscuit, Nutella-milk drizzle and a milk chocolate chip.

Preorders open on Friday, I prepare everything during the week and each box is packed ready for collection the following Friday.

This is a small home business, so there is no huge production line or endless menu. The focus is a premium cookie made carefully, available as a 4 or 6 Cookie Box, for people who fancy something genuinely good at the weekend.
